1. priority_queue 复杂度分析:单次 log(size)
    ->堆排序 $$\log(n!) = \log(\sqrt{2\pi n} (\frac{n}{e})^n) = n \log(\frac{n}{e}) + \log(\sqrt{2 \pi n}) \sim O(n\log n)$$
    (斯特林公式)
